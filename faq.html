Frequently Asked Questions

What is CODE RED'25?

+

CODE RED'25 is a 25-hour national hackathon that brings together developers, innovators, and designers to build solutions for real-world problems.

When and where will CODE RED'25 be held?

+

CODE RED'25 will be conducted on 9th and 10th January 2025 at BMS Institute of Technology and Management (BMSIT), Bangalore.

Who can participate in the hackathon?

+

Only college students, irrespective of their branch or year of study, are eligible to participate.

How many members are allowed in a team?

+

Teams can consist of 3 to 4 members. Each participant can join only one team and cannot be part of multiple teams.

Are there any pre-requisites for participation?

+

Basic knowledge of programming, design, or domain expertise is beneficial but not mandatory. Bring your innovative ideas and enthusiasm!
